United Flight Insights

This project analyzes United Airlines flight delays to identify the most problematic airports and routes. Using R (tidyverse, dplyr, ggplot2), we found that Denver International Airport (DEN) experiences the highest number of departure delays, with the Denver to Chicago O’Hare (ORD) route being the most affected. The analysis explores contributing factors such as weather disruptions and operational staffing issues. Key recommendations include improving staffing levels and strengthening coordination with air traffic control to mitigate delays and enhance customer experience. This project demonstrates statistical analysis, data wrangling, and visualization skills—all relevant to consulting and analytics roles.
